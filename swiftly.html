PROJECT OVERVIEW
Swiftly is primarily a development project: it was born out of my desire to learn how to code. I learned the lifecycle of iOS development with Swift and Xcode, debugged and rewrote lines of code over many many hours, and finally shipped Swiftly to the App Store in 2018. Check it out here.
KEY FUNCTIONS: CREATE, SWIPE, COMPLETE
Create tasks, set reminders, and complete your goals to earn points. Organize your to-do list in whatever way makes the most sense.

Some Notes
CREATING SWIFTLY WITH CORE DATA
In addition to using UIKit, autolayout and animations, this application allows for data persistence with Core Data. Swiftly promotes offline usage and encourages you to manage your tasks anywhere with your iOS device.
