Hi there, I'm Nicole!
I'm a digital designer based in LA. I work closely with visual systems, language, and code.

I graduated from UC Berkeley, where I studied urban planning and solved product challenges with Berkeley Innovation, a human-centered design agency. Most recently I was at the Koreatown Youth & Community Center (KYCC), connecting the multiethnic and generational Koreatown community with online Covid-19 resources. Previously I've worked with Beyond, O/M Studio, and NPR.
